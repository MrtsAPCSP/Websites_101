Course Overview

AP Computer Science Principles introduces students to the breadth of computer science through problem solving, programming, algorithms, and the impact of computing on society.

Course Modules

  1. Variables and Data Types
  2. Conditions, Booleans, and If/Else Statements
  3. Functions
  4. Lists and Dictionaries
  5. Loops
  6. ADV Functions and Semester Project
  7. Databases & Analysis
  8. Iteration and Testing
  9. Create Task
  10. Polishing and Exploration
  11. MCQ Bootcamp
  12. FRQ Review and Presenations

AP Exam & Create Task

AP Exam: The written exam includes multiple-choice questions on programming concepts, data, the internet, and computing impacts.

Create Performance Task: Students design and develop a program of their choice, submit their code, video, and written responses through the AP Digital Portfolio.
